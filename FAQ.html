♡ shipping & timelines

once your items arrive at our overseas sorting centers, shipments from thailand usually take about 10–15 days to reach myanmar. shipments from japan typically take about 20 daus. please note that customs clearances can occasionally add slight delays.

we operate two secure receiving hubs: our thailand warehouse is located in pathumthani, and our japan warehouse is located in saitama. all your delicate parcels are received and consolidated there before making their way home.

once your parcel arrives in yangon, we will notify you immediately. we offer local pickup options, or we can carefully dispatch your package via trusted local couriers (like royal express or wepotz) to anywhere in the country.

♡ payments & pricing

we offer a wide variety of ways to clear your invoice. locally, we accept kbz pay, wave pay, uab pay, and aya pay. for international or regional transfers, we also accept bank of ayudhya, remitly, wechat pay, union pay, and alipay.

to secure your items immediately from overseas sellers, we usually require the base cost of the item to be cleared upfront. international shipping and final local delivery fees can be settled once the items safely reach our yangon house.

because market values fluctuate, our mmk to thb and jpy conversion rates are updated regularly to reflect the current landscape. the rate locked in at the moment you submit your manifest is the rate you will be charged.
