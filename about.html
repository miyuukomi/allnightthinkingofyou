♡ the origins

all night thinking of you named after the classic 2024 vocaloid song by TOKOTOKO, was originally conceived as personal project for just a few friends. it was created for the moments when distance feels a little too wide, and the things we want feel just a little too far out of reach.

we wanted to create a safe, aesthetic space in myanmar where you can effortlessly get your hands on the items you've been dreaming about from japan and thailand.

our service is ran by a first year business administration student, so we apologize (more like i apologize) if there was delay in responses.

♡ our philosophy

we don't just see this as logistics. we see it as handling little pieces of joy, and every item is treated with love no matter how small it is..

as a student-run service, we promise transparent pricing, gentle communication, and a service that feels less like a transaction and more like a favor from a friend.
